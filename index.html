LIFE EXPECTANCY PREDICTION REGRESSION ANALYSIS AND MODEL

I employed R for an in-depth data analysis, utilizing key packages such as "MASS," "car," "leaps," and "psych." In the process, I conducted comprehensive residual analysis and variable transformations to optimize the performance of the statistical models. Addressing multicollinearity concerns, I utilized correlation matrices and Variance Inflation Factor (VIF) assessments to identify and mitigate potential issues. For variable selection and model refinement, I employed stepwise regression, ensuring a streamlined and effective model. To validate the model's assumptions, I conducted various diagnostic checks, including assessments of residual histograms, QQ plots, and residuals versus fitted plots. These diagnostic checks confirmed the model's adherence to assumptions of linearity and homoscedasticity, enhancing the robustness and reliability of the analytical outcomes.
